**Crossed Wrenches (X-Shape)**

- Symbolizes problem-solving capability
- Creates memorable, symmetrical design

**Dual Smartphones**
- Represents our core service focus
- Modern, relatable imagery
- Appeals to tech-savvy customers

**Rounded Corners**
- Friendly and approachable personality
- Modern design aesthetic
- Ensures clarity at any size (16px to 512px)
